# Model Training

How robot policies are trained: **teleops**, **behavior cloning**, **reinforcement learning**.

---

## Overview

- **Teleops** — Human-in-the-loop data collection; minimum schema and quality.
- **Behavior cloning** — Supervised learning from demonstrations; covariate shift and mitigations.
- **Reinforcement learning** — Rewards, policy gradient, PPO; sim and sim-to-real.
